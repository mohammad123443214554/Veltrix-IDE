Our Mission

Veltrix IDE is a high-performance cloud development platform owned and developed by Mohammad Khan. Founded in February 2026, our mission is to provide a flexible, powerful, and accessible coding environment for developers worldwide.

The Platform

Veltrix IDE runs entirely in your browser — no installations, no configurations. From the first line of code to a deployed project, we give you everything you need in a clean, distraction-free environment. Our editor supports HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with live preview out of the box, with more languages coming soon.
