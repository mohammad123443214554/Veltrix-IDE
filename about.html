Our Mission

Veltrix IDE is a high-performance cloud development platform owned and developed by Mohammad Khan. Founded in February 2026, our mission is to provide a flexible, powerful, and accessible coding environment for developers worldwide.

The Platform

Veltrix IDE runs entirely in your browser — no installations, no setup, no friction. From a blank file to a deployed project, we give you everything you need in a clean, focused environment. Our editor supports HTML, CSS, and JavaScript with live preview, with more languages planned.

The Founder

Veltrix IDE was founded and is actively developed by Mohammad Khan, a software engineer with a passion for developer tooling and accessible technology. Every feature in Veltrix IDE exists to reduce friction and let developers focus purely on building.

Design Philosophy

We believe great developer tools should be simple, fast, and beautiful. Veltrix IDE uses a strict grayscale design system across all four professional themes. No distractions. No clutter. Just you and your code.

What's Coming

We're actively developing multi-file project management, real-time collaboration, and support for additional languages including Python, TypeScript, and more. Follow our updates and be among the first to access new features.
